# Download ClaimsCo Document Tools

## For new staff or fresh installs

[Download Latest Version](https://github.com/nicholasgoss1/documentnamingapp/releases/latest/download/ClaimsCo_Tools_Setup_latest.exe)

This is the only link you need. Bookmark it.

## For existing staff

You do not need to download again unless you see a notification
in the app status bar saying "App update available".

AI improvements reach your app automatically and silently
every time you open it. No downloads needed.

## What the two release types mean

| Release type | Do I need to download? |
|---|---|
| App Update vX.X.X | Yes — contains new features or fixes |
| AI Improvement Update | No — delivered silently on app open |
